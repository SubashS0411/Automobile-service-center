Service FAQs

Everything you need to know about our maintenance process.

How long does a general service take?

A standard service usually takes 3-4 hours. We offer express servicing for certain packages that can be completed in under 90 minutes.

Do you provide pickup and drop-off?

Yes, we offer complimentary pickup and drop-off within a 15-mile radius of our service center for all premium members.

What happens if additional repairs are needed?

We provide a digital health report with photos/videos of any issues found. We only proceed after your explicit digital approval via our app.

Is my vehicle insured during the service?

Absolutely. All vehicles in our care are fully insured against any accidental damage from the moment of pickup to delivery.
